Lasagna

Description

A recipe for the World's best lasagna from the website Allrecepies. This recipe for lasagna is filled with all sorts of meats and is covered in freshly grated parmesan cheese

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
    • Dried Basil Leaves
    • Parsley
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
    • Parmesan
    • Mozzarella
    • Ricotta
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
