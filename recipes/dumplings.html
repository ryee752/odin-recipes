Dumplings

Description

A recipe for dumplings from the website Allrecepies. Dumplings are typically filled with all sorts of meats and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Baking Powder
  • Flour
  • Salt
  • White Sugar
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cheeses

Steps

  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl
  2. Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly
  3. Stir in milk and mix until a batter forms that is thick enough to be scooped with a spoon
  4. Allow batter to rest for 3 to 5 minutes
  5. Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup. Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes
